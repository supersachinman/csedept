The Mission of the department is as follows:

1. The Department is committed to nurture talents of students for research and innovation and excellence in the field of computer engineering starting from Under graduate level.

2. The Department of Computer Science & Engineering is committed to develop socially sensitive, highly analytical and qualified computer engineers with professional ethics by imparting training on cutting edge technology.

3. The department has designed its program to meet the requirements of the global industry.

4. Department focuses on R&D environment in close partnership with industry and foreign universities.

5. The department intends to produce well-rounded, up to date, scientifically tempered, design oriented engineer and scientists capable of lifelong learning.
